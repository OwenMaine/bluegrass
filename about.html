About Bluegrass Carpentry

Founded with a passion for craftsmanship and structural integrity, Bluegrass Carpentry has been serving South Central Kentucky for over 20 years. We specialize in structural repairs, home restoration, and custom carpentry solutions that stand the test of time.

Our team of skilled professionals brings expertise in foundation repair, beam replacement, floor leveling, and comprehensive structural solutions. We take pride in our attention to detail and commitment to using the highest quality materials for every project.

What sets us apart is our dedication to honest service and reliable workmanship. We treat every home as if it were our own, ensuring that each repair not only meets but exceeds industry standards.
